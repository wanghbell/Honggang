Academic profile

Engineering practical intelligence for the connected world.

Dr. Wang’s work spans AI and machine learning, wireless communications, multimedia systems, cybersecurity, autonomous vehicles, and smart connected health. His research turns emerging technologies into real-world systems that improve safety, privacy, and quality of care.

An alumnus of the National Academy of Engineering Frontiers of Engineering program, he has led major IEEE journals and technical committees while mentoring more than 30 graduate students, including 12 Ph.D. graduates.

“We’re not building AI for its own sake. We’re building systems that solve real problems.”
